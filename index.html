CONHACKS 2026: SKY'S THE LIMIT

Welcome to ConHacks 2026, the annual hackathon hosted by the Conestoga Computing Society (CCS). For 36 hours, we are turning the Waterloo Campus into a retro-futuristic dreamscape. Whether you are new to coding or a full-stack veteran, this is your platform to learn, build, and create.

Hacking Challenge: Open
No specific theme, build whatever you dream up!

WHY JOIN?

Beginner Friendly

Our "Starter Pack" workshops will get your environment set up in minutes and ready to code anything. Mentors will be available to help you glide through any problems.

Free Food & Swag

Participation gets you free food for the entire 36 hours (breakfast, lunch, dinner, snacks, coffee, and more). Plus ConHacks 2026 themed merch, stickers, etc.

Career Boost

Connect with fellow hackers, sponsors, mentors, and the CCS team. Get a project to display on your resume or portfolio.

Learn & Apply

Apply classroom concepts to real projects. Build, experiment, and learn by doing in a chill, lo-fi environment designed for flow state.

REQUIREMENTS

To qualify for judging and prizes, teams must adhere to:

  • Devpost Submission: Project page created by Sunday, 9:00 AM.
  • Code Repository: Public GitHub/GitLab link. Must be created after Fri 9 PM.
  • Video Demo: Max 3 mins. Show what it does, problem solved, and importance.
  • Project Description: Problem, solution, how you built it, challenges, what's next.
  • Team Size: 1–4 people.

The Sky's the Limit. Web app, mobile app, hardware, game, terminal tool - if it solves a problem through software, it counts.

CRITERIA

  • Technical Complexity (30%) Custom backend? Complex APIs? New stack? We want clean, functional, impressive engineering.
  • Innovation & Creativity (30%) Unique idea? Pushing boundaries? Novel solutions?
  • Design & Polish (20%) UX/UI matter. Is the design sleek, intuitive, and cohesive?
  • The Pitch (20%) 3 minutes to sell your dream. Articulate the problem, solution, and impact. Demo is crucial.

RULES

Be Cool. Be Kind. Hack Fair.

  1. Code of Conduct: Strict adherence required. No harassment of any kind.
  2. Fresh Code Policy: All code must be written during the event (Fri 9 PM - Sun 9 AM). No pre-finished projects.
  3. Intellectual Property: You own what you build. CCS claims no rights.
  4. Team Size: Up to 4 members. Team formation session available.
  5. Venue Rules: Respect the campus. Clean up trash. Treat equipment with care.
